Mortgages in the UAE

UAE mortgage rates are around 4-5% in 2026 (variable, linked to EIBOR). Maximum LTV is 80% for expats (first property) and 85% for UAE nationals. Maximum loan term is 25 years. Life insurance is mandatory.

DLD Fees

The Dubai Land Department charges 4% transfer fee on all property transactions, plus 0.25% for the mortgage registration. Abu Dhabi charges 2%.

UAE Specifics

No income tax, no capital gains tax, no property tax in Dubai — but annual service charges (maintenance fees) can be significant (AED 10-30/sq ft).

❓ FAQ

What is the maximum LTV in UAE?▾
80% for expat first property, 70% for expat second property, 85% for UAE nationals (first property under AED 5M).
Are there property taxes in Dubai?▾
No annual property tax. But you pay 4% DLD transfer fee at purchase and annual service charges (maintenance).
